EVENING AKAZI-TEA

 

 
Discover Something Healthy and Delicious

 
 

 In 2005, Akazi-Hotel  a revolution in the fast food industry launching a unique market niche in Dubai.
 Our founder's vision was to create the first and only healthy Indian fast food takeout concept.
 Our goal was to provide a healthy and delicious meal experience for busy people 
 looking to have their food brought to them whenand where they want it.
 After more than a decade and over 1 million baked samosas later,
 Veda has expanded from takeout roots, to focusing on home and office delivery,
 and now private, campus, and corporate catering.
 Pop in to one of our three downtown Toronto locations, 
 or even better, let us bring the food to you!
